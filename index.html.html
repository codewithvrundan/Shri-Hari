Our Heritage

A Legacy Forged in Gold

"Every ornament we create carries the soul of a tradition that spans generations."

Founded over two decades ago, Shri Hari began as a humble atelier dedicated to preserving the art of handcrafted Indian jewellery. Our master craftsmen, trained in age-old techniques, blend traditional Polki, Kundan and Meenakari work with contemporary sensibilities.

From bridal trousseau to everyday elegance, each piece is a testament to our commitment — zero compromise on purity, craftsmanship, and trust.

We have proudly served over 10,000 families across Gujarat and beyond, becoming a name synonymous with heritage, quality, and the promise of timeless beauty.
